Seychelles

Seychelles is an island country and archipelagic state consisting of 155 islands. Seychelles was uninhabited prior to being encountered by Europeans in the 16th century. Seychelles is home to some of the world's most unique and fascinating wildlife. With over 100 species of birds and a variety of marine life, the Seychelles is a nature lover's dream come true. . Since proclaiming independence from the United Kingdom in 1976, it has developed from a largely agricultural society to a market-based diversified economy, characterized by service, public sector, and tourism activities. From 1976 to 2015, Seychelles as been a wonderland for many to visit.
