Overview:

Namibia, located in southern Africa, boasts stunning landscapes, diverse wildlife and rich cultural heritage.

Must-Visit Attractions:

  • Etosha National Park: Wildlife haven with salt pans
  • Namib Desert: Stunning sand dunes and sunsets
  • Mount Pahia: Hike to the summit for breathtaking views
  • Swakopmund: German-influenced coastal town
  • Fish River Canyon: Breathtaking canyon views

Things to do

  • Safari: Explore Etosha and other national parks
  • Hiking: Discover Namib Desert's hidden gems
  • Cultural tours: Learn about Himba and San cultures
  • Adventure sports: Skydiving, sandboarding and more/li>
  • Star gazing: Enjoy crystal-clear night skies

Cuisine:

  • Braai: Traditional barbecue
  • Sosaties: Skewered meat dishes
  • Potjiekos: Hearty stew
  • Mopane worms: Local snack
