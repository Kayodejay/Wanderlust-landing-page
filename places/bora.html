Overview:

Bora Bora is a small South Pacific island in the society Islands archipelago, renowned for its:

  1. Crystal-clear turquoise lagoon
  2. Overwater bungalows
  3. Stunning white-sand beaches
  4. Coral reef diving and snorkeiling
  5. Luxurious resorts

Must-Visit Attractions:

  • Matira Beach: A picturesque public beach with calm waters
  • Mount Pahia: Hike to the summit for breathtaking views
  • InterContinental Resort's Sea Turtle Sanctuary
  • Ancient Temple of Marae Taputapuatea

Things to do

  • Snorkeling and diving
  • Sunset cruises, Island hopping
  • Shark and ray feeding
  • 4WD safari tours, Helicopter tours
  • Water sports (jet-skiing, parasailing, kayaking)

Fun Facts:

  • Bora Bora is a popular honeymoon destination
  • The island's iconic overwater bungalows were invented here
